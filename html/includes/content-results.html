How It Works

/

Take advantage of our wide range of experiences, aptitudes and insights.

Prospects searching online for available rural land or homes see digital ads promoting loans from their local Farm Credit Association. When they click on an ad, they’re taken to a landing page uniquely branded for that Association where they can either request contact from a loan officer or download a guide to purchasing equipment for country living.

People who download the guide become leads, who then receive a series of short, informative emails over the next 30 days with an invitation to connect with a Farm Credit loan officer. Those who do request contact exit the drip campaign and enter a “request for contact” workflow.

To date, the Rural Home and Land Campaign has resulted in several closed loans, as well as promising new leads being channeled into the pipeline. We’re continuing to refine our strategy based on feedback from participating Associations and by monitoring key performance indicators.
