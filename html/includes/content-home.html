Farm Market iD Lead-Generation Campaign

Purpose

Postcard campaign targeting farm owners and operators within the AgFirst District pulled from the Farm Market iD agricultural database.

Farmers within participating Association territories receive a series of postcards that promote loan products specifically tailored for rural living and agriculture. Calls to action encourage postcard recipients to get in touch with a loan officer or visit an Association landing page for more information.

Goals:

  • Develop a District-wide lead-generation platform by building a digital channel that serves as a centralized content repository.
  • Drive consumer awareness and leads through targeted conversations rather than through cold calls.
