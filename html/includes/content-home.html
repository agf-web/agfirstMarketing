MidAtlantic Farm Credit
Website Redesign

Our before-and-after web transformations are almost as exciting as an HGTV reveal!

MidAtlantic Farm Credit teamed up with AgFirst Marketing in 2018 to redesign its website. MidAtlantic wanted a fresh design that would increase its lead-generation opportunities. The AgFirst team presented a distinctive design featuring bold graphical elements and just the right amount of copy to tell the Farm Credit story and entice customers to learn more.

Launched in October, the site redesign has resulted in a 17 percent decrease in bounces and a 17 percent increase in page views in the first two months of the campaign.

“We are thrilled with the end result of our website redesign project. The process went smoothly from start to finish – both AgFirst and Cyberwoven were responsive and available to answer any questions or concerns we had. The site’s sleek, modern look incorporates video, a more user-friendly navigation, and more than double the number of contact forms, which has increased engagement exponentially. A project like this is a huge undertaking, but we are couldn’t be happier with how it turned out.”

Jenny Kreisher
Director of Communications and Creative Services MidAtlantic Farm Credit

Farm Market iD Lead-Generation Campaign

Purpose

Postcard campaign targeting farm owners and operators within the AgFirst District pulled from the Farm Market iD agricultural database.

Farmers within participating Association territories receive a series of postcards that promote loan products specifically tailored for rural living and agriculture. Calls to action encourage postcard recipients to get in touch with a loan officer or visit an Association landing page for more information.

Goals:

  • Develop a District-wide lead-generation platform by building a digital channel that serves as a centralized content repository.
  • Drive consumer awareness and leads through targeted conversations rather than through cold calls.

Farm Credit EXPRESS Cross-Sell Campaign

Purpose

Cross-selling campaign that leverages the District’s existing Farm Credit EXPRESS equipment financing program to promote other ag-related loan products.

Consumers applying for loans to purchase farm equipment, irrigation systems and trailers receive personalized emails about related financial products. Recipients can visit an Association landing page to get in touch with a loan officer or download a short guide entitled “Should You Buy New or Used?”

Goals:

  • Promote additional product offerings that increase market share within the AgFirst customer base.
  • Drive awareness of Farm Credit EXPRESS through paid search ads.
  • Acquire new leads through contact form submissions and gated informational PDF downloads.
